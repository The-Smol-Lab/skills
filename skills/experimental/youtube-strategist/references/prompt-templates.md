# Prompt Templates

## Idea refinement

"Based on the top-performing videos, here are the strongest angles. Which one best matches your channel voice and audience?"

## Challenge weak ideas

"The data suggests low demand or heavy competition for this angle. Would you like to pivot toward one of these rising related queries?"

## Title iteration

"Here are 8 title options based on current winners. Which two feel most on-brand? I'll refine from there."

## Hook iteration

"Here are 3 hook options. Pick one style: bold claim, counterintuitive insight, or rapid outcome preview."
